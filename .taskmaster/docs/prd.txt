BonsAI – Your AI-powered Plant-Based Food Assistant

The meal planner is the core product. Everything else (scanner, recipe conversion, grocery planner) becomes features that increase retention and justify a subscription.

PRODUCT REQUIREMENTS DOCUMENT (PRD)
Project Name: BonsAI
Tagline: Grow Smarter. Eat Better.

Vision
Build the world's smartest AI-powered assistant for plant-based eating.
BonsAI helps users save time, save money, reduce food waste, and eat healthier by combining AI meal planning, intelligent shopping, ingredient analysis, recipe conversion, and nutrition into one seamless experience.

Goals
Primary Goals:
- Create personalized meal plans in under 30 seconds
- Reduce meal planning effort by 90%
- Reduce grocery spending
- Reduce food waste
- Help users discover new meals
- Build recurring monthly subscription revenue

Success Metrics:
User: Daily Active Users, Weekly Meal Plans Generated, Recipes Cooked, Grocery Lists Created, Scanner Uses, Recipe Swaps, User Retention (30 days)
Business: 100 → 1,000 → 10,000 paying users
Subscription: £7.99/month, Annual £69/year

Target Audience
Primary: Plant-based eaters, Vegetarians, Vegans, Flexitarians, Families, Busy professionals, Students, Fitness enthusiasts
Secondary: People reducing meat consumption, Budget-conscious shoppers, Health-conscious users

User Personas
Busy Professional: Quick dinners, grocery list, healthy food
Family: Budget, kid-friendly meals, batch cooking
Gym User: High protein, calories, macro tracking
Student: Cheap meals, minimal ingredients, fast recipes

Core Features
MODULE 1 – AI Meal Planner (CORE)
Inputs: Goals (Weight Loss/Muscle Gain/Maintenance), Diet (Vegan/Vegetarian/Plant-Based), Allergies, Dislikes, Budget, Cooking Skill, Cooking Time, People, Kitchen Equipment, Available Ingredients, Preferred Supermarket, Meals Per Day, Snacks, Calories, Macros
Outputs: 7-day plan (Breakfast/Lunch/Dinner/Snacks), Shopping List, Estimated Cost, Nutrition, Cooking Instructions, Meal Prep Guide, Leftover Reuse
AI optimizes for: Nutrition, Budget, Variety, Time, Leftovers, Seasonal ingredients

MODULE 2 – AI Scanner
Upload photo → OCR → Ingredient Extraction → AI Analysis
Supported: Food labels, Menus, Ingredients, Recipes, Restaurant menus
Returns: Vegan/Vegetarian checks, Hidden animal ingredients, Allergens, Nutrition summary, Health Score, Ultra Processed Score, Better Alternatives

MODULE 3 – Vegan Swap Assistant
Convert recipes to Vegan/Vegetarian/Gluten Free/High Protein/Low Calorie/Budget/Kid Friendly variants with ingredients, instructions, nutrition, cost, shopping list

MODULE 4 – Grocery Planner
Budget + People + Days → Meal Plan + Shopping List + Estimated Spend + supermarket suggestions + substitutions
Stretch: Live pricing (Tesco, Asda, Sainsbury's, Morrisons, Aldi, Lidl, Ocado, Amazon Fresh)

MODULE 5 – Pantry
Users maintain pantry; AI uses existing food first; reduces waste

MODULE 6 – Leftover AI
Input leftover ingredients → AI creates meals

MODULE 7 – Nutrition Dashboard
Daily/Weekly/Monthly: Calories, Protein, Iron, Calcium, Vitamin B12, Omega 3, Fiber, Micronutrients

MODULE 8 – AI Coach
Daily chat for cooking advice, nutrition Q&A, shopping suggestions

MODULE 9 – Shopping List
Interactive, grouped by aisle, checkboxes, shareable, export PDF, Apple Reminders, Google Keep

MODULE 10 – Saved Recipes
Collections, Favorites, Meal History, Shopping History

AI Features: OpenAI for recipe generation, nutrition advice, meal planning, scanner analysis, recipe conversion, meal optimization, food waste suggestions

Authentication: Google, Apple, Email, Magic Link (Neon Auth)

Subscription
Free: 5 meal plans/month, 10 scans/month, 10 AI chats/day, limited grocery planner, basic recipes
Premium £7.99/month: Unlimited meal plans/scans/AI, nutrition dashboard, pantry, recipe history, smart grocery, leftover AI, priority AI
Family £14.99: Up to 6 users, shared pantry/grocery list, family meal planning

Database entities: Users, Profiles, Recipes, Meal Plans, Ingredients, Pantry, Shopping Lists, Scans, Nutrition, Subscriptions, Usage Limits, Favorites

Suggested Stack
Frontend: Next.js, TypeScript, TailwindCSS, shadcn/ui, TanStack Query
Backend: Next.js API Routes, Drizzle ORM, PostgreSQL (Neon), OpenAI Responses API, Blob storage, Cron Jobs
Auth: Neon Auth
Payments: Stripe (Checkout, Portal, Webhooks, Monthly/Yearly, Trials, Coupons)
Storage: Vercel Blob or Cloudflare R2
Deployment: Currently Netlify (existing); PRD mentioned Vercel as option

Development Roadmap for AI Agents
Phase 1 – Foundation: Next.js, TypeScript, Tailwind, shadcn/ui, Drizzle, Neon Postgres, auth, Stripe scaffolding, deployment; database schema and migrations; env/secrets/CI
Phase 2 – Authentication & Billing: OAuth/passwordless, Stripe Checkout/portal/webhooks, gate premium features
Phase 3 – User Profiles: Onboarding for dietary preferences, allergies, goals, budget, skill, equipment, household, pantry basics
Phase 4 – AI Meal Planning: Weekly plans, recipes, nutrition, shopping lists, leftovers, regenerate meals
Phase 5 – Grocery Planner: Lists from plans, budgeting, supermarket prefs, future live pricing architecture
Phase 6 – Scanner: Image upload, OCR, AI analysis for vegan status/allergens/alternatives
Phase 7 – Recipe Conversion: Convert to diet variants with substitutions and nutrition
Phase 8 – Pantry & Leftovers: Inventory management, recipes from available ingredients, waste reduction
Phase 9 – Dashboards & Analytics: Nutrition tracking, usage analytics, subscription metrics
Phase 10 – Polish & Launch: Performance, a11y, SEO, email, marketing pages, beta, production launch
